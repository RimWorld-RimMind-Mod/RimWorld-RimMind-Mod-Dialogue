# RimMind - Dialogue

让殖民者开口说话，为游戏事件注入生动的 AI 生成对话。

## 核心能力

**事件响应对话** - 当殖民者升级技能、获得特性、陷入恋爱、精神崩溃时，自动生成符合情境的内心独白或对话。

**玩家主动对话** - 右键点击殖民者，选择"对话"选项，主动发起聊天，了解他们的想法和感受。

**对话回复链** - A 对话后自动触发 B 的回复，让社交互动自然流动。

**对话历史记忆** - 维护多轮对话上下文（默认保留最近 6 轮），让对话有连贯性，而非每次从头开始。

**思想注入系统** - AI 生成的对话以 Thought 形式注入游戏，影响殖民者心情，让对话有实际游戏效果。

**对话浮窗** - 屏幕角落实时显示最近对话，不错过任何精彩瞬间。

## 触发场景

- 技能升级时的自我评价
- 获得新特性时的心态变化
- 社交互动后的感想
- 健康状态变化时的反应
- 玩家主动询问时的心声
- 空闲时的随机独白

# RimMind - Dialogue (English)

Make your colonists speak, injecting vivid AI-generated dialogue into game events.

## Key Features

**Event-Responsive Dialogue** - Automatically generates context-appropriate inner monologues or conversations when colonists level up skills, gain traits, fall in love, or have mental breaks.

**Player-Initiated Dialogue** - Right-click a colonist and select "Dialogue" to start a chat, learning their thoughts and feelings.

**Dialogue Reply Chain** - After A speaks, B automatically responds, making social interactions flow naturally.

**Dialogue History Memory** - Maintains multi-turn conversation context (default: last 6 rounds), ensuring coherent conversations rather than starting fresh each time.

**Thought Injection System** - AI-generated dialogue is injected into the game as Thoughts, affecting colonist mood and giving dialogue actual gameplay impact.

**Dialogue Overlay** - Real-time dialogue display in screen corner, never miss a moment.

## Trigger Scenarios

- Self-evaluation when leveling up skills
- Mindset changes when gaining new traits
- Reflections after social interactions
- Reactions to health status changes
- Inner voices when player asks
- Random monologues during idle time
